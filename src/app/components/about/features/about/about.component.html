ABOUT OM MIG

Originally from a hospitality background, I have decided a few years ago to pursue a career that I would be passionate about.
While I enjoyed the thrills, social aspect and multi-tasking of the reception, I lacked continuous learning, creativity and complex problem solving.
I bridged the gap with Le Wagon bootcamp and never looked back.

I would describe myself as a passionate .NET developer, currently working on a project I have co-built from the ground up with my friend and colleague Atte. We hope we can soon release this to the public. My skills extend across the entire spectrum of programming, encompassing frontend, backend, cloud and databases.
I am used to working in high paced environments and never hesitate to go a step further, achieving that “extra-mile” essential to most projects’ success.
My dedication to continuous learning and my unwavering belief in the power of teamwork make me a strong asset to any developer team.

And more than anything I love to solve a good problem!
